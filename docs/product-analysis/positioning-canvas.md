## Step 1: Competitive Alternatives

**What would customers use if TackBird didn't exist?**

Based on JTBD analysis and Finnish market research:

| Alternative | % of Users | Job It Covers | What It Doesn't Do |
|------------|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Tori.fi** | ~40% | Buy/sell used items | No lending, no community, no trust tiers, no events, no neighborhood scope |
| **Facebook Marketplace + neighborhood groups** | ~30% | Buy/sell + informal community | Fragmented (separate apps/groups), no trust system, no structured lending, algorithm-buried posts |
| **WhatsApp groups (taloyhtiö/block)** | ~15% | Informal coordination | Invite-only, unstructured, no search, no profiles, no trust, chaotic |
| **Do nothing / ask in person** | ~10% | Occasional neighbor interaction | Doesn't scale, limited to people you already know |
| **Kierrätyskeskus / kirpputorit** | ~5% | Give away / buy cheap | Physical only, no digital, no lending, no community |

**Key insight:** The biggest "competitor" is the fragmented combination of Tori + Facebook + WhatsApp. Nobody uses ONE tool for all neighborhood needs. TackBird replaces three apps with one.

## Step 2: Unique Attributes

**What does TackBird have that alternatives DON'T?**

Each passes the "Only we..." test:

| # | Unique Attribute | "Only We" Statement |
|---|-----------------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Structured peer lending with deposit protection** | Only TackBird offers structured tool/item lending with Stripe-powered deposits, daily pricing, and automated return tracking in Finland |
| 2 | **3-tier progressive trust system** | Only TackBird has a 3-tier trust system (Basic → Verified → Trusted) with ID verification, review history, and trust scores that compound over time |

| 5 | **All-in-one neighborhood platform** | Only TackBird combines marketplace + lending + events + forum + messaging + trust in a single neighborhood-scoped app |
| 6 | **Finnish-first triingual support** | Only TackBird is designed Finnish-first (fi/en/sv) for the Finnish market — not a translated American product |

## Step 4: Best-Fit Customers

**Who cares MOST about these value themes?**

### Primary: The Urban Neighbor (Liisa-persona)

| Characteristic | Value | Why Identifiable |
|---------------|-------|-----------------|
| Age | 25-40 | Core rental demographic |
| Location | Helsinki inner city (Kallio, Töölö, Vallila, Kruununhaka) | Dense neighborhoods with small apartments |
| Housing | Renter in kerrostalo (apartment building) | Limited storage → borrowing > owning |
| Values | Sustainability, community, experiences > possessions | Actively seeks alternatives to buying |
| Digital behavior | Uses Tori.fi or FB Marketplace already | Has the habit, just needs a better tool |
| Language | Finnish (comfortable with English) | Native UX resonates |

### Secondary: The Suburban Parent (Mikko-persona)

| Characteristic | Value |
|---------------|-------|
| Age | 30-45 |
| Location | Helsinki suburbs (Leppävaara, Tapiola, Herttoniemi) |
| Situation | Kids' stuff cycles constantly |

- Tourists / temporary residents (no investment in community)
- People in rural areas (not enough density)
- Pure sellers (Tori.fi is better for volume selling)

## Step 5: Market Category

### Strategy: **Subcategory** of existing

**Category:** Naapuruston oma ilmoitustaulu ja lainauspalvelu
("Your neighborhood's own bulletin board and lending service")

**Why subcategory, not new category:**
- "Ilmoitustaulu" (bulletin board) is immediately understood by every Finn
- Adding "lainauspalvelu" (lending service) is the unique twist that differentiates
- Avoids the education tax of "neighborhood social platform" (too abstract)
- Positions against Tori.fi (classifieds) and FB (social) simultaneously

**Why not head-to-head with Tori.fi:**
- Can't win on liquidity or brand recognition
- Different job entirely — community + lending vs. pure commerce

**Why not create a new category:**
- Solo founder, pre-launch — no budget for category education
- "Naapurustoalusta" means nothing to a regular Finnish person
- Leverage existing understanding, add the twist
