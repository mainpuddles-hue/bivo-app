# TackBird — User Personas

> Date: 2026-04-23 | Market: Helsinki metropolitan area, Finland

---

## Persona 1: Liisa — The Neighborhood Connector

### Demographics
- **Age:** 34
- **Location:** Kallio, Helsinki
- **Occupation:** UX designer at a tech company
- **Household:** Lives alone in a 45m² studio
- **Income:** ~3 800€/month net
- **Language:** Finnish (speaks English fluently)

### Psychographics
- Values sustainability and experiences over possessions
- Socially progressive, active in neighborhood culture
- Enjoys Sunday morning flea markets at Hietaniemi
- Follows zero-waste groups on Instagram
- Would rather borrow than own rarely-used items

### Technology
- iPhone 15, early adopter of new apps
- Uses: Tori.fi (selling), Wolt (food delivery), HSL app (transit), WhatsApp
- Comfortable with payments through apps, uses MobilePay daily
- Takes good photos, understands how to make posts attractive

### TackBird Usage
- **Primary categories:** ilmaista (giving away), lainaa (borrowing tools), tapahtuma (events)
- **Frequency:** Opens app 4-5x/week, posts 2-3 items/month
- **Peak time:** Sunday mornings (cleaning/decluttering) and Thursday evenings (weekend planning)
- **Behavior:** Posts free items from regular apartment decluttering. Borrows tools for apartment projects (drill, sander). Attends neighborhood events. Gives thoughtful reviews.

### Goals
1. Make her small apartment work — borrow instead of store rarely-used items
2. Build genuine connections in Kallio beyond just nodding at neighbors
3. Reduce waste — give usable items directly to neighbors who need them
4. Discover neighborhood events she wouldn't find otherwise

### Frustrations
1. Tori.fi buyers are unreliable (no-shows after agreeing to pick up)
2. Helsinki can feel isolating despite living in a dense neighborhood
3. Facebook groups are cluttered — hard to find what's relevant
4. No good way to know what's happening in her immediate area

### Key Scenarios
- **Giving:** Posts "Ilmaista: IKEA bookshelf, pick up from Vaasankatu. First come first served." Gets 8 messages in 20 minutes, picks the most responsive person.
- **Borrowing:** Needs a wallpaper steamer for one weekend. Searches lainaa → finds one 400m away → books for 15€/day with 50€ deposit → picks up, returns, leaves 5-star review.
- **Events:** Sees "Kallio korttelipuisto siivoustalkoot" event → joins → meets 12 neighbors → follows 3 of them on TackBird.

### Quote
> "Mä en tarvi omaa porakonetta. Mä tarvin reiän seinään."
> ("I don't need my own drill. I need a hole in the wall.")

## Persona 2: Mikko — The Suburban Parent

### Demographics
- **Age:** 41
- **Location:** Leppävaara, Espoo
- **Occupation:** Project manager at a construction company
- **Household:** Wife + 2 kids (ages 4 and 7)
- **Income:** ~5 500€/month household net
- **Language:** Finnish

### Psychographics
- Practical and time-constrained — efficiency matters most
- Kids' stuff cycles rapidly — always needing the next size up
- Active in kids' sports (jääkiekko) — knows many local parents
- Mildly concerned about screen time but relies on apps for logistics
- Trusts brands/systems, skeptical of "random people online"

### Technology
- Samsung Galaxy, not an early adopter
- Uses: K-Ruoka (groceries), WhatsApp (family/sport groups), Tori.fi (kids' stuff)
- Will use an app if it solves a real problem but won't explore "for fun"
- Prefers clear, simple interfaces — gets frustrated by complexity

### TackBird Usage
- **Primary categories:** tarvitsen (need kids' items), ilmaista (giving away outgrown clothes), tapahtuma (family events)
- **Frequency:** 2-3x/week, mostly browsing. Posts when kids outgrow things (~monthly).
- **Peak time:** Evenings after kids are in bed (20:00-21:30)
- **Behavior:** Searches for specific items. Saves searches for "lasten luistimet koko 32". Quick messaging, wants fast responses. Gives away bags of outgrown clothes.

### Goals
1. Find affordable kids' gear fast — specific sizes, near home
2. Get rid of outgrown kids' stuff to families who'll use it
3. Find local activities for the family (especially free/cheap ones)
4. Build connections with other parents in Leppävaara

### Frustrations
1. Tori.fi results are all over the metro area — doesn't want to drive 30km for a 10€ item
2. Facebook Marketplace has too many "is this available?" messages that go nowhere
3. No easy way to see what's happening for families in Leppävaara this weekend
4. Worries about safety when meeting strangers — especially for kids' events

### Key Scenarios
- **Seeking:** Posts "Tarvitsen: lasten maastopyörä 20 tuumaa, Leppävaara." Gets matched with a neighbor selling one 800m away for 80€.
- **Giving:** Bags up size 110 winter clothes → posts "Ilmaista: talvivaatteet 110-116, Leppävaara" → 15 responses in an hour → gives to the family who responds fastest.
- **Events:** Discovers "Leppävaara perheretki Nuuksioon" event → brings family → kids make friends → wife says "this is actually great."

### Quote
> "Jos se on lähellä ja helppo, mä käytän sitä. Muuten en jaksa."
> ("If it's close and easy, I'll use it. Otherwise, I can't be bothered.")

## Persona 3: Riitta — The Community Elder

### Demographics
- **Age:** 67
- **Location:** Töölö, Helsinki
- **Occupation:** Retired teacher
- **Household:** Lives alone in a 65m² apartment (husband passed 3 years ago)
- **Income:** ~2 200€/month pension
- **Language:** Finnish (basic English)

### Psychographics
- Deep sense of community responsibility and neighborhood pride
- Has lived in Töölö for 35 years — knows the area intimately
- Lonely but won't admit it; seeks purposeful social connection
- Values reciprocity — uncomfortable taking without giving
- Reads Helsingin Sanomat daily, active in taloyhtiö board

### Technology
- iPad (primary device), old iPhone for calls
- Uses: WhatsApp (family group), email, HS.fi (news)
- Will try new apps if a friend recommends them, but needs guidance
- Large text, clear buttons essential — small UI elements frustrate her
- Needs Dynamic Type support and good contrast

### TackBird Usage
- **Primary categories:** tarjoan (offering skills/help), tapahtuma (events), ilmaista (giving)
- **Frequency:** Daily browsing, posts 1-2x/week
- **Peak time:** Morning with coffee (08:00-10:00), early evening (17:00-18:00)
- **Behavior:** Offers practical help (sewing repairs, plant watering while traveling, Finnish tutoring). Organizes Töölö walking groups. Reads every forum post in her neighborhood. Gives extremely detailed reviews.

### Goals
1. Stay socially connected and useful to her community
2. Help newcomers settle into Töölö — share 35 years of local knowledge
3. Find small practical help when needed (moving furniture, tech support)
4. Discover cultural events and neighborhood gatherings

### Frustrations
1. Most apps feel designed for 25-year-olds — tiny text, confusing navigation
2. Facebook feels invasive and overwhelming — doesn't want to "be on social media"
3. No way to offer her skills/knowledge to neighbors digitally
4. Helsinki city events website is hard to navigate

### Key Scenarios
- **Offering:** Posts "Tarjoan: ompeluapua ja paikkausta, Töölö. 40v kokemus. Kahvit kuuluu hintaan." Gets 3 messages, does alterations for neighbors, builds reputation.
- **Events:** Creates "Töölönlahti kävelylenkki, joka keskiviikko klo 10" → starts with 4 people → grows to 15 regulars → becomes the social highlight of her week.
- **Seeking:** Posts "Tarvitsen: apua taulun ripustamiseen, 3. kerros Töölönkatu." A younger neighbor helps, she makes korvapuusti as thanks.

### Quote
> "Ennen tunnettiin naapurit. Nyt ei tiedetä ketä rapussa asuu."
> ("We used to know our neighbors. Now we don't even know who lives in our stairwell.")

## Persona 4: Alexei — The International Newcomer

### Demographics
- **Age:** 28
- **Location:** Pasila, Helsinki
- **Occupation:** Software developer (relocated 8 months ago from Moscow)
- **Household:** Lives alone in a rental apartment
- **Income:** ~3 500€/month net
- **Language:** English (learning Finnish, A2 level)

### Psychographics
- Eager to integrate but finds Finnish culture hard to penetrate
- Misses the community feeling of his hometown
- Environmentally conscious — shipped minimal belongings, needs to furnish apartment
- Active on Reddit (r/Finland), follows expat groups
- Open to trying new things, accepting of different communication styles

### Technology
- Pixel phone, tech-savvy (developer)
- Uses: WhatsApp, Telegram, Reddit, Wolt, Google Maps
- Evaluates apps critically — will leave a bad review for poor UX
- Expects English support and good i18n

### TackBird Usage
- **Primary categories:** tarvitsen (furnishing apartment), ilmaista (picking up free items), tapahtuma (meeting people)
- **Frequency:** Heavy first 3 months (furnishing), then 1-2x/week
- **Peak time:** Weekday evenings, weekend mornings
- **Behavior:** Uses English interface. Searches extensively for free/cheap furniture. Attends every social event he can find. Leaves detailed reviews to build trust fast. Struggles with Finnish-only posts.

### Goals
1. Furnish apartment affordably from local sources
2. Meet Finnish people in a low-pressure environment
3. Learn Finnish through real-world interactions (not just Duolingo)
4. Feel like he belongs in Helsinki, not just "lives there"

### Frustrations
1. Tori.fi is entirely in Finnish — hard to navigate
2. Fins don't initiate conversation — he needs structured reasons to interact
3. Expat communities are their own bubble — wants to meet locals
4. Joining neighborhood activities requires knowing they exist

### Key Scenarios
- **Furnishing:** Filters "ilmaista" within 3km → finds a free desk in Vallila → messages in simple Finnish ("Hei! Onko tämä vielä saatavilla?") → picks up, first real interaction with a Finnish neighbor.
- **Integrating:** Joins "Pasila International Coffee Morning" event → meets 8 people including 3 Finns → exchanges numbers → gets invited to a mökki trip 3 months later.
- **Learning:** Posts "Tarjoan: help with coding/tech in exchange for Finnish conversation practice, Pasila." Gets 4 responses.

### Quote
> "I don't need another expat bubble app. I need a way to actually meet my Finnish neighbors."

## Persona 5: Sanna — The Local Entrepreneur

### Demographics
- **Age:** 38
- **Location:** Kruununhaka, Helsinki (business in Hakaniemi)
- **Occupation:** Owns a small pottery studio and workshop space
- **Household:** Partner + cat
- **Income:** ~2 800€/month (variable, seasonal business)
- **Language:** Finnish (good English)

### Psychographics
- Passionate about local craft and sustainable business
- Values authenticity — hates corporate marketing
- Knows her regular customers by name — relationship-driven
- Active in local business association (Hakaniemi yrittäjät)
- Financially careful — every marketing euro must prove ROI

### Technology
- iPhone, uses it as primary business tool
- Uses: Instagram (business), Square (POS), Holvi (banking), Google Business
- Moderate app user — will invest time if it brings customers
- Wants analytics and proof that platform delivers customers

### TackBird Usage
- **Primary categories:** Pro business profile, tarjoan (workshops), tapahtuma (events)
- **Frequency:** 3-4x/week, manages messages and posts
- **Peak time:** Monday mornings (planning), Thursday evenings (weekend promotion)
- **Behavior:** Uses Pro profile with business hours, photos, and description. Posts workshop events. Responds to "tarvitsen: custom pottery gifts" posts. Boosts posts before holiday seasons.

### Goals
1. Attract new local customers who become regulars
2. Fill workshop classes to capacity
3. Be known as "the pottery place in Hakaniemi" — not just another Google result
4. Build authentic relationships with the neighborhood

### Frustrations
1. Instagram algorithm buries her posts — reach dropped 70% in 2 years
2. Google Ads are expensive and generic — can't target "within walking distance"
3. Can't prove ROI on most marketing — doesn't know where customers hear about her
4. Marketplace platforms feel impersonal — she's not selling commodity goods

### Key Scenarios
- **Visibility:** Has Pro business profile with studio photos, hours, location on map. When someone posts "tarvitsen: custom ceramic mug for a gift in Hakaniemi area," she appears as a recommended business.
- **Events:** Creates "Hakaniemi keraamikkapaja lauantai" event → 8 spots, sells out → participants post photos → organic word-of-mouth.
- **Boosts:** Pre-Christmas: boosts "Tarjoan: custom ceramic gifts, made in Hakaniemi" → 2 500 views in her neighborhood → 12 inquiries → 8 sales.

### Quote
> "Mun asiakas ei ole 'Helsinki 18-65'. Mun asiakas kävelee Hakaniemen torin ohi joka päivä."
> ("My customer isn't 'Helsinki 18-65'. My customer walks past Hakaniemi market every day.")

## Design Implications

| Persona Need | Design Action |
|-------------|--------------|
| Mikko wants fast, specific results | Saved searches with push notifications; distance filter prominent |
| Riitta needs large text and clarity | Ensure Dynamic Type support; high contrast; simple navigation |
| Alexei needs English | Full en translation coverage; detect language from profile |
| Sanna needs analytics | Pro dashboard: profile views, message count, boost performance |
| Liisa wants community feel | Forum, events, follow system — not just transactions |
| Everyone: trust matters | Make trust badges, reviews, and verification prominently visible |
